University Of Sharjah

The University of Sharjah is an Emirati private national university located in University City, Sharjah, United Arab Emirates. It was established in October 1997 by its founder the ruler of Sharjah Sheikh Dr. Sultan bin Muhammad Al-Qasimi to meet the emirate of Sharjah's aim of educational needs.

About The Project

This website is the main project for the interactive multimedia course, under the supervision of Dr. Muhammad Lataifa. The students were divided into two groups, the boys’ group and the girls’ group. Each group is required to create a website in HTML format as we learned in this course’s Lab. This website is the work of the boys’ group, and cooperation took place between the members until we came up with this work. You can identify the group members by clicking on the “group members” tab in the navigation. In the report that was submitted on the blackboard, you can learn more about the steps to create this site from the beginning to the end.
